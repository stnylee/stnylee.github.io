Hi, I'm Steve!

When I was 13, I taught myself HTML and Photoshop and became the kid at school with the coolest MySpace page. I've been passionate about designing for the digital experience ever since.

I see myself as a Swiss Army Knife. I'm a versatile individual with a variety of skill sets which I strive to improve everyday. My diverse work experience allows me to look at the problem holistically, and create simple, yet intuitive design that enrich people's digital experience. I love the process of brainstorming and eventually creating the solution to the problem.

When I'm not pumping out wireframes or learning how to code, you can find me at art galleries, watching a sci-fi movie, or exploring the city and nature.
