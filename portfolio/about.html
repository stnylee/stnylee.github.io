Hi, I'm Steve!

I'm a San Francisco-based multidisciplinary designer specializing in user experience.

Ever since designing my MySpace page in middle school, I've had a passion for creating experiences for the digital space. I love creating simple and intuitive designs and working on products that reach millions of users around the world.

Through the program at General Assembly, I learned so much about the fundamentals of UX design and the practical skills needed to be successful in the field. This course has enabled me to utilize my visual design, coding and business knowledge and intertwine it into the field of UX design.

When I'm not improving my coding skills or working on graphic design projects, you can find me at art galleries, watching a sci-fi movie, or exploring the city and nature.
