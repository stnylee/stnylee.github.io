Hi, I'm Steve!

I'm a San Francisco-based UX designer who strive to create simple, yet intuitive design that brings improvements to the world.

Ever since coding my MySpace page in middle school, I've had a passion for designing for the digital space. My curiosity and thirst for knowledge and self-improvement pushes me to grow and take on new challenges everyday.

When I'm not pumping out wireframes or learning how to code, you can find me at art galleries, watching a sci-fi movie, or exploring the city and nature.

Although my portfolio is still unfinished, you can still browse my work and please feel free to say hi as well!
